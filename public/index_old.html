Artas
PHP ORM project
Why Artas
Today, we have 3 choices : Doctrine, Propel and Eloquent.
Other ones are dead, or too much integrated in a framework to be used in your project.
We love Doctrine, it's an awesome ORM, with a lot of features. But last major version, 2.5, has been released 2 years ago.
Propel2 released his first alpha 4 years ago, no final version since.
Eloquent is based on ActiveRecord, who is a good pattern, but too much restrictive in our opinion.

For this reasons, and because we think we can do something awesome, Artas was born !
What we want to do
Performance
ORM suffers a bad reputation about performances. We want to change it !
See Doctrine hydrators benchmark for example.
Extensibility
Artas should be extensible. Really. Not just "possible".
Regular versions. Symfony roadmap for example, it's clear and you know when a fix or a feature will be published.
Install only what you need. Sometimes you don't need earth, but a stone. You should have the choice.
